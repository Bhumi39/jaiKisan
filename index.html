How to Use BHUMIIQ

image_search Photo Expert

Take a picture of any sick plant or fruit. Our AI will instantly scan the image, identify the disease, and offer a cure.

mic Voice Assistant

Can't type? Just tap the microphone and speak your farming questions in English or Hindi. The AI will answer and read it back to you!

storefront Mandi Prices

Select your State, District, and Crop to get real-time price updates direct from government markets so you never undersell your harvest.

weather_mix Weather Info

We use your phone's GPS to find your exact field and look up the hyper-local weather. Best for planning crop irrigation.
